About the school

A register kept since 1892

Graziel Royal Schools was founded on the belief that a school's worth is measured the way a house measures its own: by what it keeps a record of. We keep three things — what our students learn, how they carry themselves, and what they choose to give. That record is the shield above every doorway on our campus.

Today Graziel educates day and boarding students from foundation stage through sixth form, across four houses, in classrooms that still open onto the original 1892 quadrangle.

The motto, unpacked

Three words. Held equally.

Knowledge

A rigorous, examined curriculum across the sciences, humanities and arts — taught by faculty who still call themselves students of their subject.

Spirit

Resilience, curiosity and school pride, built through house competition, music, sport and the quiet confidence of showing up daily.

Service

Every senior student completes a term of community placement before graduation — the shield is not finished until it's given away.
